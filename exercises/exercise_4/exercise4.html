1. What is responsive web design, and how can it be achieved?

Responsive web design is an approach to designing and building websites to provide an optimal viewing experience across a wide range of devices and screen sizes. This includes desktop monitors, laptops, tablets, and mobile phones. The goal is to ensure that the website's layout and content adapt smoothly to various screen dimensions and orientations.
